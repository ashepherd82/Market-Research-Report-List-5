<p><ul><li>Magnetic Levitation</li><li>Inkjet Based 3D Bioprinting</li><li>Syringe Based 3D Bioprinting</li><li>Laser Based 3D Bioprinting</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnetic Levitation bio-printing uses magnetic fields to assemble cells into 3D structures. Inkjet Based 3D Bioprinting is a process where biomaterials are deposited onto a substrate layer by layer using inkjet technology. Syringe Based 3D Bioprinting involves extruding bioinks through a syringe to create 3D tissues. Laser Based 3D Bioprinting uses lasers to precisely position cells and materials in a 3D space. Other types of 3D bio-printing include methods such as microextrusion and laser-induced forward transfer.</p></p>
