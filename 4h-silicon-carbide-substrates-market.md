<p><strong>The 4H Silicon Carbide Substrates Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi-insulating</li><li>n-Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semi-insulating silicon carbide substrates have high resistivity due to deep level defects that trap charge carriers, making them suitable for high-power and high-frequency electronic devices. They are commonly used in communication systems, radar, and sensing applications. N-Type silicon carbide substrates have a lower resistivity and are doped with impurities like nitrogen or phosphorus to increase their conductivity. They are used in power devices, LEDs, and other electronic applications that require efficient charge carrier mobility. Both types of substrates offer distinct advantages for different applications in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
