<p><p>The 6-Lithium Enriched Glass Scintillator market can be divided into three types based on the lithium content: Natural Lithium, Depleted Lithium, and Enriched Lithium. Natural Lithium refers to glass scintillators with the standard abundance of lithium isotopes. Depleted Lithium contains lower levels of the Lithium-6 isotope, while Enriched Lithium has higher concentrations of Lithium-6. Each type of glass scintillator offers varying levels of sensitivity and efficiency in detecting radiation, depending on the specific isotopic composition of lithium present.</p></p>
