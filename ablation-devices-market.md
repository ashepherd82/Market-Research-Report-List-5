<p><p>Ablation devices market can be categorized into two main types: thermal ablation and non-thermal ablation. Thermal ablation involves the use of heat to destroy abnormal tissue, such as radiofrequency or microwave ablation. Non-thermal ablation, on the other hand, utilizes alternative energy sources like cryotherapy or ultrasound to target and remove unwanted tissue without the need for heat. Both types of ablation devices are used in various medical procedures to treat conditions like cancer, cardiac arrhythmias, and chronic pain.</p></p>
