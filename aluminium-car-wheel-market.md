<p><p>The aluminium car wheel market can be classified into three main types: casting, forging, and other. Casting involves pouring molten aluminium into a mold to create the wheel, resulting in a cost-effective and easily customizable option. Forging, on the other hand, involves shaping the wheel by applying heat and pressure, leading to a stronger and more durable product. The "other" market includes alternative methods such as flow-forming and hybrid techniques, offering unique benefits and characteristics to suit different automotive needs.</p></p>
