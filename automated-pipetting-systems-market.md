<p><ul><li>Multi-Channel</li><li>Single-Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automated pipetting systems are categorized into two main types based on the number of channels: multi-channel and single-channel systems. Multi-channel systems have multiple channels or pipette heads, allowing for the dispensing of several samples simultaneously, increasing efficiency and throughput. Single-channel systems, on the other hand, have only one channel and are typically used for more precise and accurate dispensing of individual samples. Both types of systems are used in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and research laboratories for liquid handling processes.</p></p>
