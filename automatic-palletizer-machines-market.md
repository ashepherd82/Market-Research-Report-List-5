<p><p>Automatic palletizer machines come in two main types - Robotic Type and Mechanical Type. Robotic Type palletizers use robotic arms to place products on pallets, offering flexibility and versatility in handling various products. Mechanical Type palletizers use mechanical components such as conveyors and lift tables to stack products onto pallets. Both types offer efficient palletizing solutions, but Robotic Type machines are preferred in industries that require high customization and frequent product changes, while Mechanical Type machines are commonly used in high-volume production environments.</p></p>
