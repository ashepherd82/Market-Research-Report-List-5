<p><p>Automatic titration systems are available in two main types: coulometric titration and volumetric titration. Coulometric titration involves measuring the amount of electricity needed to complete a reaction, making it highly accurate for trace analysis. Volumetric titration, on the other hand, measures the volume of a reagent needed to complete a reaction, making it suitable for a wide range of applications. Both types of automatic titration systems offer precise and efficient methods for analyzing samples in various industries.</p></p>
