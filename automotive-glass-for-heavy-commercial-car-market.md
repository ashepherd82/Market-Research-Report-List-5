Tempered glass is heat-treated to increase strength and shatter resistance. Laminated glass consists of two layers of glass with a layer of polyvinyl butyral sandwiched in between, providing added safety and impact resistance. Other types of automotive glass may include acoustic glass for reducing noise transmission and heated windshield for defrosting purposes. Each type offers specific benefits and features to cater to different needs in the heavy commercial car market.</p></p>
