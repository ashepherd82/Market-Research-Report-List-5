<p><p>Automotive noise filters are used to suppress unwanted electromagnetic interference in vehicles, ensuring optimal performance of electronic systems. Digital filters use algorithms to process and block out specific frequencies of noise, while analog filters use passive components like resistors and capacitors to reduce interference. Digital filters offer higher precision and flexibility, while analog filters are more cost-effective and simpler to implement. Both types of filters play a crucial role in improving the overall quality and reliability of electronic systems in vehicles.</p></p>
