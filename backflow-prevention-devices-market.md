<p><p>Backflow prevention devices market consists of various types such as Check Valves, Reduced Pressure Zone Devices, Atmospheric Vacuum Breakers, Pressure Vacuum Breakers, and others. Check valves allow water to flow in one direction only, while Reduced Pressure Zone Devices and Pressure Vacuum Breakers prevent backflow by maintaining lower pressure zones. Atmospheric Vacuum Breakers prevent backflow by allowing air to enter the system. Other types of backflow prevention devices include Double Check Valves and Air Gaps, each serving a specific purpose in protecting water supply systems.</p></p>
