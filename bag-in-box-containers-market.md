<p><ul><li>LDPE Type</li><li>EVA Type</li><li>EVOH Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bag-in-box containers are available in three main types: LDPE (low-density polyethylene), EVA (ethylene-vinyl acetate), and EVOH (ethylene-vinyl alcohol). LDPE containers are commonly used for packaging liquids due to their flexibility and durability. EVA containers have excellent impact resistance and are often used for volatile liquids. EVOH containers have superior oxygen barrier properties, making them ideal for products that need to be protected from oxidation. Each type caters to different packaging needs and offers unique properties for preserving the freshness and quality of the contents.</p></p>
