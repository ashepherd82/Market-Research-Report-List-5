<p><p>Biological plant activators are available in various forms such as water-dispersible and water-soluble granules, solutions, and wettable powders. Water-dispersible and water-soluble granules are easy to disperse in water for application. Solutions are liquid forms that can be directly applied to plants. Wettable powders are finely ground solid particles that can be mixed with water to create a suspension for spraying. Each form offers different benefits and application methods, catering to the diverse needs of farmers and agricultural professionals.</p></p>
