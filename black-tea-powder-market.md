<p><p>Black tea powder market can be categorized into two types based on solubility: hot water soluble and cold water soluble. Hot water soluble black tea powder dissolves easily in hot water, making it suitable for hot beverages like tea. Cold water soluble black tea powder, on the other hand, can dissolve in cold water without the need for heat, making it convenient for preparing cold beverages like iced tea. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and usage occasions, providing versatility in applications.</p></p>
