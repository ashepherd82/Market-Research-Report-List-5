<p><p>Booster pumps for agriculture come in two main market types: single stage and multiple stage pumps. Single stage pumps are designed with a single impeller and are suitable for low to moderate pressure applications. Multiple stage pumps, on the other hand, have two or more impellers for increased pressure output, making them ideal for high-pressure irrigation systems or for pumping water over long distances. Both types of pumps play a crucial role in improving water delivery and efficiency in agricultural operations.</p></p>
