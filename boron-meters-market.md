<p><p>Boron meters are categorized into two main types: Tank Sensor Boron Meters and Surface Sensor Boron Meters. Tank Sensor Boron Meters are used to measure boron levels in liquid inside tanks, providing accurate readings for industrial and research purposes. On the other hand, Surface Sensor Boron Meters are designed to measure boron levels on surfaces, such as soil or skin, offering valuable information for agricultural, medical, and environmental applications. Both types of boron meters play a crucial role in monitoring and maintaining boron levels in various settings.</p></p>
