<p><p>Central venous catheters come in various types such as single-lumen, double-lumen, and triple-lumen. Single-lumen catheters have one tube for infusion or withdrawal of fluids. Double-lumen catheters have two lumens for simultaneous administration of medications or fluids. Triple-lumen catheters have three lumens for additional functions like blood sampling. There are also other types of central venous catheters available in the market that cater to different medical needs and requirements, offering a wide range of options for healthcare professionals and patients.</p></p>
