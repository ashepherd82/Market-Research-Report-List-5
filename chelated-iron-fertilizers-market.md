<p><p>Chelated iron fertilizers are available in two main market types: solution and powder. Solution chelated iron fertilizers are liquid-based products that are easily soluble in water, making them convenient for foliar application or fertigation. Powder chelated iron fertilizers, on the other hand, are dry formulations that need to be dissolved in water before application to the soil. Both types of chelated iron fertilizers are effective in correcting iron deficiencies in plants and promoting healthy growth.</p></p>
