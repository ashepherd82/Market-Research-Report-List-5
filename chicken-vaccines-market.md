<p><p>Chicken vaccines market can be categorized into two main types: live vaccines and killed vaccines. Live vaccines are made from weakened or attenuated forms of the virus, providing a more robust immune response in chickens. On the other hand, killed vaccines are made from inactivated virus particles, which are safer but may require additional doses for effective protection. Both types play a crucial role in preventing various infectious diseases in poultry and improving overall flock health.</p></p>
