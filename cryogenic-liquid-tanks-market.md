The market for these tanks can be categorized into two main types: vacuum powder insulation type and atmospheric pressure powder insulation type. Vacuum powder insulation tanks use a vacuum layer between the inner and outer walls for extra thermal insulation, while atmospheric pressure powder insulation tanks use a powder material to insulate the tank. Both types are crucial in industries like healthcare, food processing, and aerospace.</p></p>
