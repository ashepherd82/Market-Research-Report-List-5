<p><p>Cryogenic triple-offset valves are available in two main types - flange type and butt welding type. Flange type valves are designed to be connected to pipes using flanges, providing a secure and leak-proof connection. Butt welding type valves, on the other hand, are welded directly to the pipe, minimizing the risk of leaks. Both types are specifically built to withstand extreme cold temperatures and harsh conditions commonly found in cryogenic applications.</p></p>
