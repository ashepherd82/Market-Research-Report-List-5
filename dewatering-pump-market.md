<p><p>The dewatering pump market can be categorized into two main types: Submersible dewatering pumps and Non-submersible dewatering pumps. Submersible dewatering pumps are designed to be placed directly into the water or fluid that needs to be removed, while non-submersible dewatering pumps are meant to be placed outside of the water source and use suction to remove excess fluids. Both types of pumps are essential in various industries for managing water levels and preventing flooding.</p></p>
