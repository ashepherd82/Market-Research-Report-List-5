<p><p>Digital Fiber Optic Amplifiers are used to enhance the signal strength of optical fibers in data transmission networks. Single Channel Output amplifiers focus on boosting the signal of a single channel, providing an efficient solution for networks with a single transmission line. Two Channel Output amplifiers, on the other hand, are designed to boost the signal of two channels simultaneously, catering to networks with dual transmission lines. Both types offer targeted amplification for specific network configurations, optimizing signal quality and transmission efficiency.</p></p>
