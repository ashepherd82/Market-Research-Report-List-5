<p><p>Electrocardiographs are medical devices used to monitor the electrical activity of the heart. The market for these devices can be classified into three main types - Resting System, Stress System, and Holter Monitor System. Resting systems are used for standard ECG tests, stress systems are used during physical activity to monitor the heart's response, and Holter monitor systems are portable devices that continuously record the heart's activity over a period of time. Each type serves a specific purpose in diagnosing and monitoring heart conditions.</p></p>
