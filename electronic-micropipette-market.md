<p><p>Electronic micropipettes are used in laboratories for accurate and precise liquid transfer. Single-channel electronic micropipettes allow for dispensing of one sample at a time, while multi-channel micropipettes can dispense multiple samples simultaneously. Single-channel micropipettes are typically preferred for tasks that require high precision, such as PCR and sequencing, while multi-channel micropipettes are more efficient for high-throughput experiments that involve multiple samples. Both types offer advantages in terms of accuracy, speed, and ease of use for various laboratory applications.</p></p>
