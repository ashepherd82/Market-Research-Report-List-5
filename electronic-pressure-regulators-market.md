<p><ul><li>Single-stage Electronic Pressure Regulators</li><li>Multi-stage Electronic Pressure Regulators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronic Pressure Regulators are categorized into two main types: Single-stage and Multi-stage. Single-stage Electronic Pressure Regulators are designed to maintain a constant output pressure regardless of fluctuating input pressures, making them ideal for applications where precise pressure control is essential. On the other hand, Multi-stage Electronic Pressure Regulators utilize multiple pressure control stages to provide a wider range of output pressures as well as greater accuracy and stability, making them suitable for more complex and demanding industrial processes.</p></p>
