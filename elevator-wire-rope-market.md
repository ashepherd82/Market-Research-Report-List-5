<p><p>The elevator wire rope market is segmented into two main types: fiber core and steel core. Fiber core wire ropes consist of synthetic materials like polypropylene or sisal, providing flexibility and resistance to corrosion. Steel core wire ropes are made of steel strands, offering higher tensile strength and durability. Both types are widely used in the elevator industry, with fiber core ropes being preferred for lighter loads and steel core ropes for heavier loads and longer service life.</p></p>
