<p><p>Endotoxin testing system market offers various types of testing methods including turbidimetric, chromogenic, rFC assays, gel clot, and others. Turbidimetric method measures the decrease in light transmission caused by the formation of endotoxin-lysate complex. Chromogenic method utilizes a color change reaction to detect endotoxins. rFC assays use a recombinant factor C protein to detect endotoxins. Gel clot method involves the formation of a gel when endotoxins are present. Other methods may include cell-based assays or immune-based assays for endotoxin detection.</p></p>
