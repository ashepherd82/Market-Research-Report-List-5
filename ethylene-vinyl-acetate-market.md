<p><p>Ethylene Vinyl Acetate (EVA) Market Types are categorized based on the vinyl acetate (VA) content in the polymer. EVA with VA content up to 4% is used in packaging materials and as a binder in asphalt. EVA with VA content between 4 to 30% is used in footwear, solar panel encapsulation, and medical devices. EVA with VA content greater than 40% is used in adhesives, coatings, and specialty films. Each market type offers unique properties and applications based on the VA content.</p></p>
