Ammonium phosphate is a source of both phosphorus and nitrogen, essential for plant growth. Ammonium sulfate is a sulfate-based nitrogen fertilizer. Other types of fertilizer additives may include potassium-based additives, micronutrients, and specialty fertilizers tailored for specific plant needs. These additives play a crucial role in enhancing soil fertility and promoting plant growth.</p></p>
