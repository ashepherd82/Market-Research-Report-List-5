<p><p>Float bimetallic steam traps are commonly found in two market types: Thread type and Flange type. Thread type steam traps are designed to be easily installed into piping systems with threaded connections, providing a convenient and secure fit. On the other hand, Flange type steam traps are installed using flanges, which offer a more robust and durable connection suitable for high-pressure applications. Both types of steam traps efficiently discharge condensate and prevent steam loss, contributing to energy savings and improved performance in steam systems.</p></p>
