<p><p>A forklift counterweight is a heavy weight attached to the rear of a forklift to help balance the weight of the load being carried on the front of the machine. This counterweight prevents the forklift from tipping over and ensures stability while lifting and transporting heavy objects.</p><p>The forklift counterweight market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.5% during the forecast period, driven by the increasing demand for material handling equipment in industries such as manufacturing, logistics, and construction. Manufacturers are increasingly using advanced materials such as steel alloys and composite materials to reduce the overall weight of the counterweight without compromising on strength and stability.</p><p>Another trend in the market is the integration of advanced technologies such as sensors and telematics in forklift counterweights to improve safety and efficiency.
