<p><p>Freight elevators are used to transport goods and materials, with two main types dominating the market: Hydraulic Freight Elevators and Electric Freight Elevators. Hydraulic freight elevators operate using a hydraulic pump to raise and lower the elevator car, while electric freight elevators use an electric motor for the same purpose. Both types serve a wide range of industries and applications, providing efficient and safe transportation of heavy items within buildings. The choice between hydraulic and electric freight elevators usually depends on factors such as load capacity, speed, and building design.</p></p>
