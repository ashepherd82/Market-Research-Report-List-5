<p><p>Glass Fiber Complex Materials Market includes two main types: Thermoset Composites and Thermoplastic Composites. Thermoset composites are materials that undergo a chemical reaction during the manufacturing process, permanently setting their shape. They are rigid and have high strength and heat resistance. Thermoplastic composites, on the other hand, can be reshaped and remolded when heated, making them more flexible and easier to recycle. Both types of composites are used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, construction, and sports equipment.</p></p>
