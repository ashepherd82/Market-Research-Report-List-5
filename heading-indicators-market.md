<p><p>Heading indicators are devices used in aviation to display the aircraft's heading. There are two main market types for heading indicators: analog and digital. Analog heading indicators display the heading using a physical pointer on a rotating compass card. Digital heading indicators, on the other hand, use electronic displays to show the heading numerically. While analog indicators are more traditional and require manual adjustment, digital indicators are becoming more popular due to their accuracy and ease of use.</p></p>
