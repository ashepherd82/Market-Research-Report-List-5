<p><strong>The HF Electrotome Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unipolar Type</li><li>Bipolar Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HF Electrotome market consists of two main types: Unipolar and Bipolar. Unipolar electrotomes use a single electrode to deliver high-frequency electrical current for cutting tissue, while the patient is the second electrode. They are mainly used for cutting soft tissues. On the other hand, Bipolar electrotomes use two electrodes to deliver electrical current, eliminating the need for a patient return electrode. They are suitable for delicate procedures where precision is key. Both types cater to different surgical needs and are widely used in the medical field.</p></p>
