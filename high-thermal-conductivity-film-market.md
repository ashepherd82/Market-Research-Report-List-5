<p><p>High thermal-conductivity film market can be divided into two main types - non-silicone film and silicone film. Non-silicone film is typically made of materials like polyimide or epoxy resin and offers high thermal conductivity, making it suitable for applications where heat dissipation is crucial. On the other hand, silicone film is made of silicone-based materials and also possesses high thermal conductivity properties, but with added benefits like flexibility and durability. Both types of films play a vital role in industries where efficient heat transfer is essential.</p></p>
