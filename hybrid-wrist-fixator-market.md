<p><p>Hybrid Wrist Fixator devices come in two main types: Round and L Shape. Round fixators consist of circular rings connected by straight wires, providing stable fixation and allowing for multi-planar adjustments. L Shape fixators feature an L-shaped frame design, offering additional stability and control over the wrist alignment. Both types are commonly used in orthopedic surgeries to treat wrist fractures and deformities, providing customizable solutions to meet the specific needs of each patient.</p></p>
