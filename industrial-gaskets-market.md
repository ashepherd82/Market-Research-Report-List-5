<p><p>Industrial gaskets are used to create a seal between two surfaces in industrial applications. They are categorized into three main types: metallic, non-metallic, and semi-metallic. Metallic gaskets are made from materials like stainless steel and are highly durable. Non-metallic gaskets are made from materials such as rubber or cork and are resistant to chemicals and high temperatures. Semi-metallic gaskets combine the strengths of both metallic and non-metallic gaskets, making them suitable for a wide range of industrial applications.</p></p>
