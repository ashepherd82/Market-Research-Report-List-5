<p><p>Industrial panel controls potentiometers are used in various applications, with two main types being manual and motorized controls. Manual potentiometers are operated manually by turning a knob or slider to adjust the resistance. They are commonly used in applications where the settings need to be adjusted frequently by an operator. Motorized potentiometers, on the other hand, are controlled electronically and can be adjusted remotely or automatically. They are often used in applications where precise and consistent adjustments are required, or where accessibility is limited.</p></p>
