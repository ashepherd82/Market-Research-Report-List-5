<p><p>IPM pheromones market is segmented into three main types - sex pheromones, aggregation pheromones, and others. Sex pheromones are chemicals released by one organism to attract another for mating purposes. Aggregation pheromones are used to attract multiple individuals of the same species to a specific location. Other types of pheromones may include alarm pheromones, trail-following pheromones, and anti-aggregation pheromones. These pheromones play a key role in Integrated Pest Management strategies by disrupting mating behaviors and reducing pest populations.</p></p>
