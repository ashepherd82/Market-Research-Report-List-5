<p><strong>The IR Heaters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Wave or Near Infrared</li><li>Medium Infrared</li><li>Far Infrared</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IR heaters are classified into three main types based on their wavelength: short wave or near infrared, medium infrared, and far infrared. Short wave or near infrared heaters emit high intensity heat with a short wavelength, making them suitable for quick heating applications. Medium infrared heaters have a longer wavelength and provide more gentle heat, making them ideal for comfort heating. Far infrared heaters emit low intensity heat with a longer wavelength, which penetrates deep into objects, making them suitable for therapeutic and wellness applications.</p></p>
