<p><p>Ladle furnaces are available in two main types, DC furnaces and AC furnaces. DC furnaces use direct current to generate heat, providing precise control over temperature and power consumption. Both types of ladle furnaces are widely used in steelmaking and other metal processing industries to refine and control the composition of molten metal before casting.</p></p>
