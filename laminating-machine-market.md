<p><ul><li>Coating Laminating Machine</li><li>Pre-coated Laminating Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laminating machines are commonly classified into two main types in the market - Coating Laminating Machines and Pre-coated Laminating Machines. Coating Laminating Machines apply a layer of adhesive or resin to the material before laminating, allowing for customization and flexibility in the laminating process. On the other hand, Pre-coated Laminating Machines use pre-coated laminating films or pouches that already have adhesive applied, making the laminating process more efficient and convenient for users. Both types cater to different preferences and requirements in the laminating industry.</p></p>
