<p><strong>The LLDPE Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C4- LLDPE</li><li>C6- LLDPE</li><li>C8- LLDPE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) is categorized based on the type of comonomer used in its production, with C4-, C6-, and C8-LLDPE being the most common types. C4-LLDPE is produced using butene as a comonomer, C6-LLDPE uses hexene, and C8-LLDPE uses octene. These different types of LLDPE have varying properties and applications, with C4-LLDPE generally being more flexible, C6-LLDPE offering improved strength and puncture resistance, and C8-LLDPE providing better clarity and heat sealability.</p></p>
