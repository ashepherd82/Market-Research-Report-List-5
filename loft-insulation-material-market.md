<p><p>Loft insulation materials can be categorized into five main types: Expanded Polystyrene (EPS) and Extruded Polystyrene (XPS), Rockwool, Polyisocyanurate (PIR) and Polyurethane (PU), Glasswool, and Other materials. EPS/XPS are lightweight and cost-effective options. Rockwool offers excellent thermal and acoustic insulation properties. PIR/PU provide high thermal performance with minimal thickness. Glasswool is a popular choice due to its affordability and ease of installation. Other materials include natural fibers and recycled materials, offering sustainable and eco-friendly insulation options.</p></p>
