<p><p>Low-voltage power transformers are classified into two main types: single phase and three phase power transformers. Single phase power transformers are designed for smaller electrical loads and typically used in residential settings, while three phase power transformers are utilized for larger industrial or commercial applications. Single phase transformers have one primary and one secondary winding, while three phase transformers have three primary and three secondary windings. Both types play a crucial role in converting voltage levels to ensure safe and efficient electricity distribution.</p></p>
