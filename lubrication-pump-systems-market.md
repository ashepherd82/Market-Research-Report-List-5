<p><p>Lubrication pump systems can be classified into three main types: manual systems, electric systems, and pneumatic systems. Manual systems require human effort to operate, making them suitable for smaller applications. Electric systems are powered by electricity and provide consistent lubrication flow, ideal for automated machinery. Pneumatic systems use compressed air to deliver lubricant, offering precise control over lubrication flow. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific needs of the application.</p></p>
