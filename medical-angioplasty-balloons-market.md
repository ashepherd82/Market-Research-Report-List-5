<p><ul><li>Normal Balloons</li><li>Drug Eluting Balloons</li><li>Cutting Balloons</li><li>Scoring Balloons</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical angioplasty balloons are used to treat blocked arteries. There are several types available in the market. Normal balloons are standard inflatable devices used to widen the artery. Drug eluting balloons are coated with medication to prevent re-narrowing of the artery. Cutting balloons have small blades on the surface to help break up tough blockages. Scoring balloons have tiny grooves that help create controlled tears in the plaque, allowing for better expansion of the artery. Each type of balloon offers unique benefits for patients undergoing angioplasty procedures.</p></p>
