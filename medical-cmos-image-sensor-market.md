<p><strong>The Medical CMOS Image Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FSI</li><li>BSI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical CMOS Image Sensor Market consists of two main types: Front-Side Illuminated (FSI) and Back-Side Illuminated (BSI). FSI sensors have the light-sensitive pixels located on the front side, resulting in lower sensitivity and efficiency compared to BSI sensors. BSI sensors have the pixels located on the back side, allowing for better sensitivity and performance in low-light conditions. Both types are used in medical imaging devices to capture high-quality images for diagnostic purposes.</p></p>
