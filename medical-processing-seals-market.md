Gaskets are flat seals typically used between two surfaces to prevent leakage. Lip Seals are dynamic seals that prevent contamination in medical equipment by forming a barrier between moving parts. Each type of seal serves a different purpose but all are essential in maintaining the integrity of medical processing equipment and ensuring the safety of patients.</p></p>
