<p><ul><li>Non-woven Medical Textile</li><li>Knitted Medical Textile</li><li>Woven Medical Textile</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical textiles can be categorized into three main types: non-woven, knitted, and woven. Non-woven medical textiles are made by bonding fibers together, providing a cost-effective and disposable option for medical applications. Knitted medical textiles are produced by interlocking yarns to create a soft and stretchable fabric, suitable for garments and bandages. Woven medical textiles are made by weaving yarns together, offering high durability and strength for surgical implants and prosthetics. Each type serves different purposes in the medical field based on their unique characteristics and properties.</p></p>
