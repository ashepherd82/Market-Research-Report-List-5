<p><p>Module Substrates Market is divided into two main types: Thin Film Substrate and Thick Film Substrate. Thin Film Substrates are used in applications requiring high precision, such as semiconductors and photovoltaics. These substrates offer excellent uniformity and control over film thickness. On the other hand, Thick Film Substrates are used in applications like sensors and actuators, where a thicker layer is needed for improved durability. These substrates are typically made of ceramic materials and provide better heat dissipation and mechanical strength.</p></p>
