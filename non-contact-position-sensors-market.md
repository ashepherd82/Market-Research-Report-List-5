<p><p>Linear position sensors and rotary position sensors are types of non-contact position sensors used in various industries. Linear position sensors measure the linear position of an object relative to a fixed point, while rotary position sensors measure the angular position of an object around a central axis. Both types of sensors provide accurate and reliable position feedback without touching the object being measured, making them ideal for applications where contact with the object may not be possible or desirable. These sensors are widely used in automotive, aerospace, robotics, and industrial automation industries.</p></p>
