<p><p>NOx Reduction Catalyst market is divided into two main types: Honeycomb Type and Flat Type. Honeycomb Type catalysts consist of a series of channels formed in a honeycomb structure, providing a large surface area for reactions to occur. This type is commonly used in diesel engines for optimal NOx reduction. On the other hand, Flat Type catalysts have a flat surface with a coating of precious metals for catalytic reactions. Both types are essential for reducing harmful nitrogen oxide emissions in various industrial applications.</p></p>
