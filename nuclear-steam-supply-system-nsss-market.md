<p><p>The Nuclear Steam Supply System (NSSS) market includes Pressurized Water Reactors (PWR), Boiling Water Reactors (BWR), and Pressurized Heavy Water Reactors (PHWR). PWRs use pressurized water to transfer heat from the nuclear fuel to the steam generators. BWRs directly produce steam in the reactor. PHWRs use heavy water as a moderator and coolant. Each type of reactor has its own set of advantages and challenges, leading to different market dynamics and preferences based on factors such as safety, efficiency, and cost.</p></p>
