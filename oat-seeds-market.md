<p><p>The oat seeds market includes two main types: common seed oats and naked oats. Common seed oats are typically covered with a husk or hull, which must be removed before consumption. They are commonly used for livestock feed. Naked oats, on the other hand, have a thinner or completely absent husk, making them easier for human consumption.
