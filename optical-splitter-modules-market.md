<p><strong>The Optical splitter Modules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fused Biconic Tapered Splitters (FBT)</li><li>Planar Lightwave Circuit Splitters (PLC)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical splitter modules are used in fiber optic networks to divide the optical power among multiple paths. Fused Biconic Tapered Splitters (FBT) use two fibers fused together to split the signal, making them cost-effective for smaller-scale applications. Planar Lightwave Circuit Splitters (PLC) use a waveguide to split the signal, offering higher performance and reliability for larger networks. Other types of optical splitter modules include Micro Optics Splitters and Beam Splitter Cubes, which provide unique solutions for specialized applications requiring precise power division and signal distribution.</p></p>
