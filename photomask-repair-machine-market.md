Different types of technologies are utilized in these machines, including laser technology, focused ion beam (FIB) technology, and nanomachining technology. Laser technology uses a high-powered laser to remove or deposit material on the photomask. FIB technology uses a beam of focused ions to repair defects on the mask. Nanomachining technology utilizes extremely small cutting tools to make precise repairs on the photomask.</p></p>
