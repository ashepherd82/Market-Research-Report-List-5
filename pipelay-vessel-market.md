<p><strong>The Pipelay Vessel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>J-lay Barges</li><li>S-lay Barges</li><li>Reel-lay Barges</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pipelay vessel market types consist of J-lay Barges, S-lay Barges, and Reel-lay Barges. J-lay Barges use a tower structure to lower pipes into the seabed at a steep angle. S-lay Barges lay pipes in an S-shape pattern, suitable for shallower waters. Reel-lay Barges use a spooling system to unroll and install pipes on the seabed. Each vessel type has specific characteristics that cater to different depths and conditions, providing flexibility for pipeline installation projects in the offshore oil and gas industry.</p></p>
