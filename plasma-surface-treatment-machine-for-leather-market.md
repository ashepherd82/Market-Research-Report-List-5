<p><p>Plasma surface treatment machines for leather can be categorized into two main types: atmospheric plasma and vacuum plasma. Atmospheric plasma treatment is conducted at ambient pressure, making it a more cost-effective and versatile option for treating leather surfaces. On the other hand, vacuum plasma treatment involves operating in a controlled vacuum environment, allowing for more precise and customized treatment processes. Both types of plasma treatment offer unique advantages in terms of improving adhesion, cleaning, and modifying the surface properties of leather materials.</p></p>
