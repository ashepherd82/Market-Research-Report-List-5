There are two main types of technologies used in these analyzers: Optical Detection and Mechanical Detection. </p><p>Optical Detection utilizes light-based technology to measure clot formation in the blood sample, while Mechanical Detection relies on physical mechanisms such as springs or beads to detect clotting.
