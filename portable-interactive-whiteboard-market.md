<p><p>Portable interactive whiteboards are available in two main market types: front projection and rear projection. Front projection whiteboards use a projector mounted in front of the board, resulting in a bright and clear display. Rear projection whiteboards have the projector mounted behind the board, offering a seamless and shadow-free display. Both types of whiteboards are portable, allowing for easy transportation and setup in various settings such as classrooms, conference rooms, or training centers.</p></p>
