<p><p>The Portable RF Signal Generator market consists of two main types: Analog Signal Generator and Vector Signal Generator. Analog Signal Generators create continuous waveform signals for testing and troubleshooting analog communication systems. They are simpler and more cost-effective than Vector Signal Generators, which can generate complex digital modulation signals for testing modern communication systems. Vector Signal Generators offer higher precision and flexibility compared to Analog Signal Generators, making them essential for industries that rely on advanced digital communication technologies.</p></p>
