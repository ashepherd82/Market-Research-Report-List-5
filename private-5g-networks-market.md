<p><p>Private 5G Networks can be categorized into two main market types based on the frequency bands they operate on - Sub-6 GHz and mmWave. Sub-6 GHz networks utilize frequencies below 6 GHz, providing wider coverage and better penetration through walls, making them suitable for indoor and outdoor deployments. On the other hand, mmWave networks operate in much higher frequency bands, offering ultra-fast speeds but limited coverage and penetration capabilities, making them ideal for high-density urban areas or specific industrial applications that require high data rates.</p></p>
