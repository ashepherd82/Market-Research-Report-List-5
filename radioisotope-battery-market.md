<p><strong>The Radioisotope Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Conversion Type</li><li>No-Thermal Conversion Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radioisotope battery market types can be divided into two categories: thermal conversion type and no-thermal conversion type. Thermal conversion type batteries convert the heat generated by the decay of radioisotopes into electricity using thermoelectric materials. On the other hand, no-thermal conversion type batteries use the radiation emitted by the radioisotopes to directly generate electricity through processes like the photovoltaic effect. Both types of batteries have their own advantages and applications in various industries such as space exploration, military equipment, and medical devices.</p></p>
