<p><p>Rectangular Hollow Section Steel is available in two main types: Welded RHS and Seamless RHS. Welded RHS is created through the welding of steel sheets to form a rectangular shape, offering a cost-effective option for various construction projects. On the other hand, Seamless RHS is manufactured without any seams, providing a smoother finish and higher strength. Both types of RHS are widely used in construction, infrastructure, and manufacturing industries for a wide range of applications.</p></p>
