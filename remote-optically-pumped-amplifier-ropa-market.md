<p><p>Remote Optically Pumped Amplifiers (ROPAs) are used in optical communication systems to amplify optical signals over long distances. Two main market types for ROPAs include Same Fiber Remote Optically Pumped Amplifiers and Foreign Fiber Remote Optically Pumped Amplifiers. Same Fiber ROPAs are suitable for applications where the amplifier is in the same fiber as the signal, while Foreign Fiber ROPAs are used when the amplifier is in a different fiber from the signal. Both types have specific use cases depending on the requirements of the optical communication system.</p></p>
