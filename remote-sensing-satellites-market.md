<p><p>Remote sensing satellites are classified into three main types - SATCOM (satellite communication) satellites, space-based radars, and space-based EO/IR (electro-optical/infrared) satellites. SATCOM satellites are used for communication purposes, allowing users to transmit data across large distances. Space-based radars are used to collect information on ground conditions and monitor changes in the surface. Space-based EO/IR satellites use sensors to capture images and detect heat signatures, providing valuable information for various industries such as agriculture, defense, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
