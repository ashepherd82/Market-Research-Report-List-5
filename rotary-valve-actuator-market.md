<p><p>Rotary valve actuators are available in four main types: pneumatic, hydraulic, electric, and others. Pneumatic actuators use compressed air to operate the valve, offering simplicity and reliability. Hydraulic actuators utilize fluid pressure for smooth and precise valve control. Electric actuators are powered by electricity and provide efficient and responsive operation. Other types of actuators may include manual or hybrid systems. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
