<p><p>Rubber shock absorbers come in various types like mechanical, hydraulic, pneumatic, and others. Mechanical shock absorbers use springs and dampers to absorb and dissipate shock energy. Hydraulic shock absorbers use fluid to dampen vibrations and absorb shock. Pneumatic shock absorbers use air or gas for cushioning impact. Other types may include electromagnetic shock absorbers or a combination of different technologies. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of efficiency, durability, and performance in various applications.</p></p>
