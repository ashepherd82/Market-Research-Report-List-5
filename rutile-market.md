<p><p>Rutile is a mineral composed of titanium dioxide, with two main market types being natural rutile and synthetic rutile. Natural rutile is found in nature and is typically used in the production of ceramics, pigments, and refractory materials. Synthetic rutile, on the other hand, is produced through the upgrading of ilmenite or other titanium feedstocks. It is used in the manufacturing of titanium dioxide pigments, titanium metal, and welding electrodes. Both natural and synthetic rutile play important roles in various industries.</p></p>
