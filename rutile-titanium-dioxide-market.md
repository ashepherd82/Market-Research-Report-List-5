<p><p>Rutile Titanium Dioxide market is categorized into two main types based on the production process: Sulfate Process and Chloride Process. The Sulfate Process involves treating titanium ores with sulfuric acid, resulting in a lower production cost but slightly lower quality product. On the other hand, the Chloride Process utilizes chlorine as the reagent, leading to a higher purity product with better optical properties. Each process has its own advantages and is used in various applications within the Rutile Titanium Dioxide market.</p></p>
