<p><p>Screw press market is categorized into two main types – Single Screw Press and Twin Screw Press. Single screw press consists of a single rotating screw that compresses materials, making it simpler and more cost-effective for smaller scale operations. On the other hand, twin screw press features two intermeshing screws that provide higher efficiency and capacity, making it suitable for larger scale operations that require more thorough compression of materials. Both types offer unique advantages depending on the specific needs of the operation.</p></p>
