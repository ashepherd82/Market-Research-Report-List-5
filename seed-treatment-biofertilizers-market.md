<p><p>Seed treatment biofertilizers are categorized into two main types: Liquid biofertilizers and carrier-based biofertilizers. Liquid biofertilizers are in a ready-to-use liquid form that can be easily applied to seeds or soil. Carrier-based biofertilizers are mixed with a carrier material, such as clay or vermiculite, which helps in the easy application and distribution of the biofertilizer. Both types of biofertilizers provide essential nutrients to enhance plant growth and yield, promoting sustainable agriculture practices.</p></p>
