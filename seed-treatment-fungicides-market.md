<p><p>Seed treatment fungicides are categorized into various types based on their application methods and formulations. Seed dressing fungicides are applied directly to the surface of seeds, providing protection against fungal diseases. Seed coating fungicides are mixed with a coating material and applied as a thin layer around the seed. Seed pelleting fungicides are incorporated into a pellet that is then applied to the seed. Other types of seed treatment fungicides may include combinations of different application methods or unique formulations for specific purposes.</p></p>
