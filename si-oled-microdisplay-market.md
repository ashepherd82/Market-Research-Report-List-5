<p><strong>The Si-OLED Microdisplay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-Luminous Type</li><li>Transmissive Type</li><li>Reflective Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Si-OLED Microdisplay market is divided into three types based on the display technology: Self-Luminous Type, Transmissive Type, and Reflective Type. The Self-Luminous Type microdisplays emit light from each pixel, providing bright and vivid images. Transmissive Type microdisplays use a backlight to illuminate the pixels, resulting in good energy efficiency. Reflective Type microdisplays use ambient light to reflect off the pixels, offering low power consumption and high contrast ratios. Each type has its unique advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
