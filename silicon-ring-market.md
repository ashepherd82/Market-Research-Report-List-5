<p><p>Silicon ring market is primarily categorized into three types based on resistivity levels. Low resistivity silicon rings are ideal for applications requiring high conductivity, such as in electronics and semiconductor industries. Medium resistivity rings find their use in a wide range of applications that require balanced conductivity and resistance properties. High resistivity silicon rings are mainly used in applications where insulation and high resistance properties are essential, such as in power distribution systems and high voltage equipment. These different types cater to various industry requirements and applications.</p></p>
