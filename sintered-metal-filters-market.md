Medium porosity filters range from 30% to 60%, balancing strength and filtration capacity. High porosity filters have a porosity above 60%, providing maximum filtration capacity but lower strength. These different types cater to various filtration requirements in industries such as automotive, chemical, and pharmaceutical.</p></p>
