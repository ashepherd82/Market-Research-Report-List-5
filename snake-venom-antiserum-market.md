<p><p>Snake venom antiserum is classified into two main types: polyvalent and monovalent. Polyvalent antiserum is effective against a broad range of snake venoms, making it a versatile option for treating snakebites from various species. On the other hand, monovalent antiserum is specific to the venom of one particular snake species. While polyvalent antiserum is more commonly used due to its broader coverage, monovalent antiserum may be preferred in certain situations where the specific snake species is known. Both types play a crucial role in the treatment of snakebites.</p></p>
