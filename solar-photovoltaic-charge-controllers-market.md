<p><p>Solar Photovoltaic Charge Controllers Market Types include PWM Type and MPPT Type. PWM Type controllers are more cost-effective and are suitable for smaller solar systems. They work by rapidly switching the power supply to the battery on and off. On the other hand, MPPT Type controllers are more efficient and can increase the overall power output of a solar system by tracking and adjusting the voltage and current to maximize power production. They are ideal for larger solar systems.</p></p>
