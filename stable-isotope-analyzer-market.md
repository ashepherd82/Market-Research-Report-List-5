<p><p>Stable Isotope Analyzer market includes two main types: Stable IRMS and Laser-based analyzers. Stable IRMS (Isotope Ratio Mass Spectrometry) analyzers measure the isotopic composition of elements in a sample by separating ions based on their mass-to-charge ratio. Laser-based analyzers use laser technology to analyze stable isotopes in a sample. Both types of analyzers are commonly used in a variety of industries such as environmental science, food & beverage, and forensic analysis for their high precision and accuracy in measuring isotopic composition.</p></p>
