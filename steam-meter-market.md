<p><strong>The Steam Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vortex Flowmeters</li><li>Differential-pressure Flowmeters</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Steam meter market types include Vortex Flowmeters, Differential-pressure Flowmeters, and Others. Vortex Flowmeters measure steam flow by detecting the frequency of vortices shed from a bluff body in the flow path. Differential-pressure Flowmeters calculate steam flow by measuring the pressure drop across a constriction in the flow path. Other types of steam meters may include Ultrasonic Flowmeters, Turbine Flowmeters, and Coriolis Flowmeters. Each type of meter offers unique advantages and is suitable for various applications in steam flow measurement.</p></p>
