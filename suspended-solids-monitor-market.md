<p><p>Suspended solids monitors are utilized in various industries to measure the levels of suspended solids in liquids. The market can be categorized into two main types: Submersible Probe and Insert Probe. Submersible Probe monitors are designed to be submerged directly into the liquid, providing real-time data on suspended solids levels. On the other hand, Insert Probe monitors are inserted into a pipe or tank to measure suspended solids. Both types offer accurate and reliable monitoring solutions for different applications.</p></p>
