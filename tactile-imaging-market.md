The sensor composed of pressure-sensitive material is able to detect and measure pressure exerted on its surface, providing detailed information about touch and pressure. On the other hand, traditional conductor flexible sensor uses traditional conductive materials to sense touch and pressure, offering different benefits and applications compared to pressure-sensitive material sensors. Both types of sensors play a vital role in tactile imaging technology.</p></p>
