<p><p>TDLAS Gas Analyzers are used in two main market types: In Situ Laser Analyzer and Extractive Laser Analyzer. In Situ Laser Analyzers measure gas concentrations directly within the process, offering real-time monitoring capabilities. Extractive Laser Analyzers, on the other hand, sample gas from the process stream and analyze it in a separate location. Both types offer accurate and reliable gas analysis for a variety of industries, including environmental monitoring, process control, and emissions reduction.</p></p>
