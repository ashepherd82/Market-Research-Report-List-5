<p><p>Thermostatic Radiator Valves (TRVs) are devices that regulate the flow of hot water into a radiator to control the room temperature. There are two main types of TRVs: Self-Operate TRVs which use a wax-like substance to expand and contract based on the room temperature, and Electronics Valves which are equipped with electronic sensors and actuators to automatically adjust the flow of hot water. Both types offer energy efficiency and comfort benefits for heating systems.</p></p>
