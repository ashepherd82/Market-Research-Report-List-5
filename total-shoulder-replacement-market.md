<p><p>Total shoulder replacement is a surgical procedure where damaged parts of the shoulder joint are replaced with artificial components. There are two main types of total shoulder replacements: Anatomic total shoulder replacements involve replacing the ball and socket joint with artificial components that mimic the natural anatomy of the shoulder. Reverse total shoulder replacements involve switching the positions of the ball and socket components, which is beneficial for patients with certain types of shoulder injuries or arthritis. Both types of shoulder replacements aim to restore function and reduce pain in the shoulder joint.</p></p>
