<p><ul><li>Shunt Way</li><li>Intercellular Lipid Structure Pathway</li><li>Trans-cellular Pathway</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Transdermal patch drug delivery systems utilize different pathways to deliver medication into the body. The "Shunt Way" involves the drug passing through hair follicles or sweat glands. The "Intercellular Lipid Structure Pathway" involves the drug passing through the lipid layers of the skin cells. The "Trans-cellular Pathway" involves the drug passing through the skin cells themselves. Each of these pathways offers unique advantages and challenges for drug delivery, allowing for more targeted and efficient treatment delivery for various medical conditions.</p></p>
