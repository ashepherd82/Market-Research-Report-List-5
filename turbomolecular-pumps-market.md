<p><p>Turbomolecular pumps are classified based on their types including Magnetically Suspended Type, Oil Lubricated Type, and Others. Magnetically Suspended Type pumps utilize magnetic bearings to suspend the rotor, reducing friction and eliminating the need for lubricants. Oil Lubricated Type pumps use oil for lubrication and cooling purposes. Other types of turbomolecular pumps may include hybrid variants that combine various technologies for optimal performance. Each type caters to different applications and requirements within the turbomolecular pump market.</p></p>
