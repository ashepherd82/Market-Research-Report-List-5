<p><p>Ultra-fast diodes come in two main types: single tube and double tube. Single tube diodes are compact and efficient, with a single structure that allows for quick switching speeds and high frequency capabilities. On the other hand, double tube diodes have two separate structures within one housing, which provides increased power handling capabilities and improved thermal performance. Both types are commonly used in applications requiring high-speed rectification, such as power supplies, inverters, and motor drives.</p></p>
