<p><p>Ultra High Performance Liquid Chromatography (UHPLC) offers various types of chromatography techniques such as Ion Exchange Chromatography, Ion Exclusion Chromatography, and Ion Pair Chromatography. Ion Exchange Chromatography separates ions based on their charge properties, while Ion Exclusion Chromatography separates analytes by their size and shape. Ion Pair Chromatography involves the use of ion pairing agents to enhance the separation of ionic compounds. These different types of UHPLC techniques cater to different analytical needs and are widely used in various industries for high-performance separations.</p></p>
