<p><p>Ultrasonic flow meters are categorized into three main types based on their installation methods: Inline type, Clamp-On type, and Insertion type. Inline type flow meters are directly installed in the pipeline, Clamp-On type meters are attached externally without disrupting the flow, and Insertion type meters are inserted into the pipe wall or mounted into a pipe fitting. Each type offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications depending on factors such as pipe size, fluid type, and installation preferences.</p></p>
