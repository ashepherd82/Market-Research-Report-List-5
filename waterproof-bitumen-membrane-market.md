<p><p>Waterproof bitumen membrane market consists of two main types: SBS type and APP type. SBS type membranes are enhanced with styrene-butadiene-styrene materials, offering flexibility and high performance in various weather conditions. On the other hand, APP type membranes are modified with atactic polypropylene, providing better resistance to UV rays and higher temperatures. Both types are widely used in construction for waterproofing roofs, basements, and other structures due to their durability, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
