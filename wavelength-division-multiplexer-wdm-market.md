<p><strong>The Wavelength Division Multiplexer WDM Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coarse Wavelength Division Multiplexer (CWDM)</li><li>Dense Wavelength Division Multiplexer (DWDM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wavelength Division Multiplexer (WDM) market includes two main types: Coarse Wavelength Division Multiplexer (CWDM) and Dense Wavelength Division Multiplexer (DWDM). CWDM operates with a wider wavelength spacing, typically 20nm, allowing for lower cost and simpler technology. DWDM, on the other hand, uses tighter spacing, usually 0.8nm or less, enabling higher capacity and longer distances for data transmission. Both CWDM and DWDM systems are widely used in telecommunications networks for efficient multiplexing and demultiplexing of optical signals.</p></p>
