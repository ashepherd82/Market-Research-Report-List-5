<p><p>The Wind Turbine Lighting Market includes Horizontal Axis Wind Turbine (HAWT) and Vertical Axis Wind Turbine (VAWT) markets. HAWT consists of blades attached to a central hub that is positioned horizontally to the ground, while VAWT has its blades vertical to the ground. HAWT is more widely used due to higher efficiency and easier maintenance, while VAWT is gaining popularity for its ability to generate power from wind coming from any direction. Both have various lighting solutions to ensure visibility and safety.</p></p>
