<p><p>Wood primers are essential for prepping wood surfaces before painting or staining. There are three main types of wood primers available in the market: oil-based primers, water-based primers, and shellac-based primers. Oil-based primers are known for their durability and ability to seal stains and tannins. Water-based primers are eco-friendly and quick-drying, making them ideal for indoor use. Shellac-based primers offer excellent adhesion and stain-blocking capabilities. Each type of primer has its own unique advantages and is suitable for different wood surfaces and projects.</p></p>
