<p><p>Zinc pigment market can be segmented into two types based on the form of zinc used: Zinc paste and Zinc powder. Zinc paste is a thick, semi-solid material made by blending zinc powder with a binding agent. It is commonly used in protective coatings for corrosion prevention on steel structures. Zinc powder, on the other hand, is a fine, dry powder that is used in various industrial applications such as paints, rubber, and plastics for its anti-corrosive properties. Both forms of zinc pigment play a crucial role in protecting surfaces from deterioration.</p></p>
